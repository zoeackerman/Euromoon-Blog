Day 33

Saturday - 09 Jan 2016

By 1500 we have been dropped off at Disney Land and the adventure begins. We smash out the Walt Disney Studio section of the park and make plans for how we are going to spend Sunday and Monday.

The evening ends at 2100 after a spectacular light and sound show. I'm all tingly inside :-D

***Try not to sweat the small stuff.***

Day 34

Sunday - 10 Jan 2016

The park doesn't open until 1000 so we get to sleep in and enjoy our breakfast. The previous evening we had done our research and marked the map provided with all the things we wanted to do. We quickly got down to business.

Lang also go to experience my love for Stitch in full CRAZY. Every store they had I wanted a photo with the Stitch merchandise that I really really wanted but could not fit in my suitcase. Even after 5 years of dating me i'm not sure he had a clue what he was getting himself into taking me to Disney Land! *insert maniacal laugh*
